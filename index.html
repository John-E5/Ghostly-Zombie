Ghostly Zombie

An eerie feeling is overtaking the town as community members learn that more Zombie parts have gone missing. Nobody knows where they disappeared. Some believe that it is foul-play while others believe that a ghost may be involved. For days now, community members have lived in fear waiting to see what happens next. Six zombie body parts are missing so far. They include: 2 arms, 2 legs, a head, and a torso. It is believed that the missing zombie body parts are scattered at haunted places all over the world. Can you help find the Zombie body parts and bring them back home? Do you have what it takes to find the Zombie? To rescue the Zombie parts, one must answer each question correctly.
